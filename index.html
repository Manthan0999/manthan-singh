Hi, welcome to my portfolio! I am a Cloud DevOps Engineer and Software Engineer currently pursuing my Master’s in Computer Science at Stony Brook University. Over the course of my career, I’ve designed and implemented large-scale, resilient cloud solutions, specializing in AWS, GCP, and OCI platforms. From architecting multicloud CI/CD pipelines to containerizing applications with Docker and Kubernetes, I excel at weaving automation and scalability into every facet of the development lifecycle.

In my most recent role as an AWS Cloud DevOps Engineer Intern at Affinity Solutions, I spearheaded a multicloud CI/CD pipeline using Jenkins, Bitbucket, Docker, and Terraform—reducing release times by 25%. I introduced reusable Terraform modules for standardized infrastructure provisioning, integrated CloudFront-based CDNs for secure global delivery, and leveraged Helm charts on Kubernetes to boost deployment reliability and consistency at scale.

Prior to that, I worked at Deloitte USI as a Senior Cloud Infrastructure Engineer, guiding clients in adopting advanced cloud security solutions and orchestrating large-scale infrastructure automations. Highlights of this role include implementing an Istio-based service mesh on EKS for secure traffic routing, developing AutoRemediation pipelines for 300+ AWS accounts using Python and Terraform, and constructing robust cross-cloud BCDR solutions with near-zero downtime. These experiences honed my ability to deliver high-performance, fault-tolerant systems that align with stringent SLAs and security standards.

Beyond my cloud-centric roles, I have a strong foundation in full-stack development with JavaScript, React, Node.js, and Python. My passion for building seamless user experiences drives me to create scalable, responsive applications. I also enjoy exploring machine learning and leveraging data-driven techniques for continual innovation.

Ultimately, my mission is to architect forward-thinking solutions that solve real-world challenges. Thank you for visiting my portfolio! Feel free to explore, connect with me on LinkedIn or GitHub, and reach out for collaborations or discussions around cloud technology, software engineering, and innovation.
